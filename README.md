# Mechanical Systems Design + Additive Manufacturing Portfolio

Welcome to my **Mechanical Systems Design + Additive Manufacturing Portfolio**.  
This repository showcases **16 weeks of projects** combining CAD assemblies, materials study, simulation, and additive manufacturing. All projects are designed to demonstrate my **mechanical systems design skills**, **material knowledge**, **simulation capabilities**, and **3D printing expertise**.  

---

## **Skills Demonstrated**
- **CAD Design & Assemblies**: SolidWorks, Fusion 360, Siemens NX  
- **Mechanism Design & Motion Simulation**: Exploded views, joints, gear trains, robotic arms  
- **Materials & Engineering Analysis**: Material selection, FEA (stress, fatigue, deformation)  
- **Additive Manufacturing**: 3D printing design, lattice structures, lightweight optimization  
- **Python / AI for Engineering** (optional): Design optimization, predictive analysis  

---

## **Project Roadmap**
The projects are split into **two phases**:

### **Phase 1 – Core CAD & Assembly + Additive Manufacturing (Weeks 1–8)**
| Week | Project | Focus |
|------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Simple Lever & Bracket | CAD basics, part modeling |
| 2 | Spur Gear & Cam | Gear modeling, assembly mates |
| 3 | 2-Part Linkage Mechanism | Assembly motion, clearance design |
| 4 | Gear Pair Motion Study | Motion simulation, gear interaction |
| 5 | 1-DOF Robotic Arm Joint | Mechanism design, motion study |
| 6 | Lever + Cam Multi-Part Assembly | Multi-part assembly, exploded view |
| 7 | Small Gearbox Assembly | Gear trains, motion simulation |
| 8 | Portfolio Compilation & Optional Print | Documentation, screenshots, AM |

### **Phase 2 – Materials, Simulation & Additive Manufacturing (Weeks 9–16)**
| Week | Project | Focus |
|------|---------|-------|
| 9 | Material Comparison Study | PLA, PETG, ABS, stress & weight |
| 10 | Material Assignment for Robotic Joint | CAD material assignment, simulation |
| 11 | FEA on Lever / Bracket | Stress, deformation, AM preparation |
| 12 | FEA on Gearbox / Robotic Joint | Assembly stress analysis |
| 13 | Geometry Optimization | Lattice/hollow structures, lightweight design |
| 14 | Fatigue & Safety Analysis | Repeated load analysis, durability |

| 16 | Final Portfolio Project & Print | Complete robotic arm/gearbox, AM print |
